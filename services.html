Migration

We handle migrations between tools, platforms, and environments - whether you're switching databases, moving to the cloud, or modernizing your entire data stack. Migration capabilities also support our Data Warehousing and Data Lakehouse services.

Platform Migration

Move between database platforms - Oracle to Snowflake, SQL Server to Redshift, PostgreSQL to BigQuery, and more. We handle schema translation, data transfer, and validation.

Cloud to Cloud

Migrate between cloud providers - AWS to GCP, Azure to AWS, GCP to Azure. We manage the full transition including compute, storage, and networking considerations.

Local to Cloud

Move on-premise databases and infrastructure to the cloud. We plan the migration path, handle data transfer, and ensure your cloud environment is production-ready.

Warehouse to Lakehouse

Modernize your data architecture by migrating from a traditional warehouse to a lakehouse model, combining structured and unstructured data in a unified platform.

Reporting Tool Migration

Transition between reporting and BI tools - Cognos, Tableau, Power BI, Business Objects, Crystal Reports, and more. We migrate dashboards, reports, and data connections.

ETL/ELT Pipeline Migration

Move your data pipelines between tools - Informatica to dbt, SSIS to Airflow, Talend to Fivetran. We rebuild and validate pipelines to ensure continuity.

Data Transformations

Restructure and transform your data as part of any migration - ensuring data formats, schemas, and business logic carry over cleanly to the new environment.
